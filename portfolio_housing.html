Project information

  • Technology used: Tableau
  • Category: Data Visualization
  • Client type : Housing Market Analysts, Real Estate Professionals

Dataset Description

Real estate and housing market data specific to Washington.

Description of the Project

My Tableau project, 'Housing in Washington,' offers a comprehensive visual exploration of the Washington housing market. It includes key visuals such as a linear graph tracking sales prices over time, a geographic map depicting sales activity, and histograms revealing price, bedroom, and bathroom distributions. The linear graph provides historical insights into housing price trends, while the map helps identify hotspots in the market. The histograms offer a quick overview of price ranges and property configurations. Additionally, a heatmap correlates property views and conditions with prices, aiding decision-making. This project is a valuable resource for housing market analysts, real estate professionals, and those interested in Washington's real estate landscape, enabling data-driven decision-making and trend recognition in the dynamic housing market.

Goal

The purpose of this Tableau project is to provide valuable insights into the housing market in Washington, aiding housing market analysts, real estate professionals, and other stakeholders in making informed decisions related to buying, selling, or investing in properties. This project leverages Tableau's powerful visualization capabilities to present data-driven insights that can guide housing market strategies and investments in Washington.
